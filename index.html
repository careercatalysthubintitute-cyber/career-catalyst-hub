Our Story

Born From a Belief in Every Student

Career Catalyst Hub Institute was founded in 2022 by Anchal Sir, Divyansh Sir and Chouhan Sir with one powerful belief — that every student in Bilaspur deserves access to quality coaching that was previously only available in big cities like Raipur or Delhi.

Starting with just a handful of determined students, we have grown into a trusted institution that has trained over 1000 students across Class 9–12, NEET, JEE, CUET, PPHT, and PAT — with outstanding results.

Located near Nalanda Library, Mannu Chowk, Bilaspur — our institute provides a focused, inspiring learning environment where students from across the city come to achieve their dreams.
